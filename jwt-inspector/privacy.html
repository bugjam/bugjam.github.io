JWT Inspector: Privacy Policy

Once activated, the JWT Inspector Chrome extension will read network traffic sent from your browser in order to present information in the "JWT" tab in Chrome devtools.

You may optionally copy information from the "JWT" tab to the clipboard.

The JWT Inspector extension does not use your data for any other purpose than this. Your data is not sent to any server or shared with me or any 3rd party.
